AI Frameworks

The TensorFlow & PyTorch frameworks power the AI’s capability to detect patterns in blockchain data and identify potential security threats, anomalies, or vulnerabilities in smart contracts.

NYXCORE uses Anomaly Detection Algorithms which use unsupervised learning to spot unusual patterns in blockchain transactions, identifying potential attacks like double-spending, Sybil attacks, or unauthorized access.

NLP techniques are also used to analyze smart contract code, documents, and communications for any signs of malicious intent or vulnerabilities.

Quantum-Resistant Encryption

With quantum computing on the horizon, traditional encryption techniques may become vulnerable to attacks. NYXCORE AI employs quantum-resistant encryption algorithms to future-proof the security of blockchain systems.

NYXCORE Utilizes lattice-based cryptography, which is believed to be resistant to quantum attacks, to protect transactions and sensitive blockchain data.

The platform integrates post-quantum cryptography standards such as the NTRU algorithm to ensure that encryption remains secure in the post-quantum era.
